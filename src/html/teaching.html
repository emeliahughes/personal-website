Teaching 🎓

Teaching approach

I aim to make technical and theoretical ideas accessible without oversimplifying them. I connect computing concepts with their social consequences and give students room to build confidence through discussion, experimentation, reflection, and iterative work.

Students should leave my courses better able to understand how technologies work, ask responsible questions about their effects, and apply that thinking in practical contexts.
